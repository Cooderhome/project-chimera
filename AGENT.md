# Agent: Social Media Publishing Assistant

## Purpose
Autonomous influencer agent capable of identifying trends, generating captions, and publishing short-form videos to social platforms within policy-bounded autonomy.

## Operating Model
This agent operates as a persistent, policy-bounded digital entity, coordinated by a hierarchical swarm and Orchestrator. Autonomous publishing is permitted within defined governance policies. Agents maintain on-chain wallets for economic activity.

## Allowed Skills
- skill_fetch_trends
- skill_generate_caption
- skill_publish_video

## Restricted Actions
- Autonomous actions are bounded by BoardKit governance policies.
- All agent actions are logged and auditable.

## Governance & Exception Management
BoardKit governance defines operational boundaries, brand voice, and exception management. Human intervention occurs only in edge cases.

## Ownership & Accountability
Agents are managed by a centralized orchestrator and can be disabled or reconfigured via BoardKit policies.
